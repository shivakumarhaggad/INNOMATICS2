Our Story

Founded in 2010, Brewed Awakening began with a simple mission: to serve exceptional coffee in a warm, welcoming environment. We source our beans directly from sustainable farms and roast them in small batches to ensure the highest quality in every cup.

Our skilled baristas are passionate about their craft and dedicated to creating the perfect beverage for each customer. Whether you're starting your day with us, meeting friends, or looking for a cozy spot to work, we're here to make your experience memorable.
